Mayo College, Ajmer sometimes refered as "Eton of the East" was founded in 1875 by Lord Mayo, the then Viceroy of India. The college is a prestigious boarding school located in Ajmer, Rajasthan. I had the privilege of attending this amazing institution for my education.

Mayo provided me with some of the most treasured memories of my life. The school instilled in me a strong sense of discipline and camaraderie. I picked up many of my interests here, such as aeromodelling, playing violin, cricket and horse riding.

I am indebted to Mayo College for providing me with an excellent education and it will always hold a special place in my heart.

I would recommend this institution to anyone looking for a top-notch boarding school experience.
