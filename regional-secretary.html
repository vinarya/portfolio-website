As the Regional Secretary of North India for the Aeromodellers Association of India (AMAI), I have helped organise multiple seminars and National Championships all over India. This has included India's first Indoor Aeromodelling Championship which had over 400 participants. The championship lasted for 4 days and included several workshops from experts in aviation and robotics. We also hosted the President of Aero Club of India as the Chief Guest at the award ceremony. Another notable event I helped organise was the Aeromodelling Exhibition at Delhi Public School, Rudrapur. Several members of AMAI joined me in performing aerobatics with model airplanes and helicopters for an audience of 1000+ students and underprivileged children in order to expose them to the S.T.E.M field early on in their life.

I am passionate about promoting aeromodelling as a hobby and sport in India, and work tirelessly to ensure that our events are enjoyable and safe for everyone involved. I am always looking for new ways to grow the sport in India, and hope to continue doing so for many years to come.
