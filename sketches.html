I adored drawing my favorite cartoon characters and superheroes as a kid, but it wasn't until I enrolled into Mayo College that I developed an interest in drawing portraits. My art and crafts teacher Mr. Vijay Singh Chauhan, a renowned artist in India, immediately recognised my talent for drawing and took me under his wing. I would often spend my free time working on portraits of famous celebrities honing my skills and trying to get the details just right. My most treasured work is a portrait of Mr Kapil Dev, who led the Indian Cricket team to win its first World Cup. He autographed the piece when I presented it to him during his visit for our school's annual function.
