I've been playing the violin for almost ten years now, and I can say without a doubt that it's been one of the most challenging and rewarding experiences of my life. When I first started out, I was completely clueless about how to even hold the instrument, let alone play it. But with a lot of practice (and a lot of patience from my instructors), I eventually got the hang of it. I as also part of the Mayo College Symphony orchestra that played the opening act for a Vishal and Shekar (famous bollywood composers) concert at the Tata theatre in Mumbai.

After a hectic day I love to unwind by playing some soothing Indian classical music like Raags and old musical pieces. Some of my favorite songs are Toss the Feathers and Lough Erin Shore by The Corrs.
